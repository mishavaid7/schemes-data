AB-PMJAY was introduced as the "world's largest government funded healthcare program" that will provide 110 million families a cover of up to Rs. 5 lakhs per family per year, for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization.

Read more about the objectives of the scheme in our 2021 story: https://www.indiaspend.com/explainers/public-health-insurance-ayushman-bharat-health-resource-centre-healthcare-768375

As of Dec 2023, approximately 28.1 crore Ayushman cards have been created and over 6.06 crore hospital admissions worth Rs. 78,372 crore have been authorised under Ayushman Bharat
